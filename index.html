Welcome to our site! Here you will find information on running in Leeds

Reasons to run:

  1. Great way to get out, get some fresh air and see the countryside.
  2. Healthy! You increase your heart rate and burn some calories.
  3. Excellent for your mental health.
  4. Decreases risk of so many health conditions.
  5. Running is a great social event, you can make so many friends through it.
  6. Work towards a goal. Running can give your life structure and something to work towards.
Whatever reason resonates with you, get out on the road today. Don't wait. It's so easy to say "I'll do it tomorrow." But frequently there are so many reasons that pop up to not follow through. Don't worry if you're a new runner, just start slowly and build up your distance over time.
