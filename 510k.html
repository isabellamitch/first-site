Routes over 5K

These routes are all a little bit longer. The one shown below on the left starts at the Clarendon Road entrence to Woodhouse Moor. You zig-zag through the park and then complete a lap around the Hyde Park Area. The second one, on the right, is a long one. Nearly 6 miles. This route is nearly a full 10k and its great for training for a 10k or even a half marathon. It will take you all the way out past Headingley and round through Burley. All on main roads, so you are nice and safe.
